Yakitori

Yakitori is an easy to make, and delicious meal.

The simplicity of Japanese cuisine makes
simple ingedients shine on their own.
Yakitori is no exception

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sake
  • caster sugar
  • shitake mushrooms

Steps

  1. Make yakitori sauce
  2. Soak skewers
  3. Get grill going
  4. Skewer chicken and mushrooms, then glaze them in sauce
  5. Grill until ready, then enjoy
