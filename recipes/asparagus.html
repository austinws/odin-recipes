Oven Roasted Asparagus

Healthy and delicious?!

Oven roasted asparagus is super easy to make,
is super healthy for you,
and goes good with nearly any meat.

Ingredients

  • fresh stalks of asparagus
  • olive oil
  • lemon pepper
  • garlic powder
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 385 F
  2. Pour a small amount of oil on oven safe pan, set asparagus on it and put it in the oven
  3. After 5 minutes of baking, take it out and put the spices on it, to your taste
  4. Put the pan back in the oven
  5. Roast until the end of the asparagus are slightly burnt
  6. Enjoy the flavor, and the health benefits.
