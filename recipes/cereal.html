Just Plain Ol' Cereal

It's cereal, how hard can it be?

Seriously, all you do is pour two things into a bowl.

Ingredients

  • cereal of your choice (Cocoa Puffs)
  • milk of your choice

Steps

  1. Get bowl
  2. Pour cereal into bowl
  3. Pour milk into bowl
  4. Start eating it
  5. Wonder if you are too old for cereal (You aren't)
